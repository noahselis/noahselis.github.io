Welcome to Shige's

Executive Chef Shige Matsuzawa and his wife Debbie opened Shige Japanese Cuisine, the first Japanese restaurant in downtown Boise in 1992, with a vision of creating Boise's finest Japanese dining experience. After nearly 26 years in Boise, the Matsuzawa’s have taken that experience and instilled it into their current location in Meridian, Shige Sushi Express.

Quality Crafted

Shige infuses every dish he creates with his extensive knowledge and passion for perfection. He started his career as a Chef in Japan, before moving to California and then relocating to the Treasure Valley.

So Handsome

Shige Cuisine is committed to serving only the finest foods, made with the freshest ingredients. All of our dishes are made from scratch. Our traditional decor and friendly servers create a memorable dining experience. We hope to see you soon!
