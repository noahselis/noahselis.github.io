Blurb about me

Hello! My name is Noah Selis and I have lived in Boise for almost 10 years. I moved down here form Northern Idaho (almost Canada. In fact, just think of it as Canada.) for college but I clearly still had some growing up to do so I spent the next few years deciding exactly what I did NOT want to be when I grew up.

I moved around, spent money I didn't have on tacos I didn't need, adopted a dog, got married, adopted another dog, and enrolled in the software development program at CWI where I am currently studying.

I've been interested in programming for about a year but I've been all about solving problems for a dog's age. Any chance to fiddle with or pick apart a puzzle box or conundrum I'm on it like black on beans. I reckon that's what I was missing when I went to college the first time: an opportunity to solve problems in a tactile way.

I'm grateful for my lovely wife and two very important dogs for their support as I get my shit together.
